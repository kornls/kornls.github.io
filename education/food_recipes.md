# Useful Recipes that you can make at home

### 1. Kraken Stew
🐙 + 🔥 + 🍽️ = 🍲

<br>

You will need : A Kraken

<br>

**Instructions :**
1. Catch the Kraken from the seas
2. Cook the Kraken at medium heat
3. Serve, and enjoy!
